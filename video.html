Clock Maps

Search for a realm or map name, and select the map of your choice.

Controls
Mouse wheel or "Zoom" button to zoom in and out.

While zoomed in, click and drag on the map to pan around.

Drag opacity slider for desired transparency.

Click and drag on toggle tab or header area to move up or down.

Click on the tab again to collapse to the side while remembering choices.

Click on the X to collapse to the side and reset selections.
