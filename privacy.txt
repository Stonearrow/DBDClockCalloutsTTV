1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Dead by Daylight Clock Maps (“Extension,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information in connection with your use of the Twitch Extension.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information from users.

Specifically:

We do not collect Twitch User IDs

We do not collect names, email addresses, or profile information

We do not collect location data

We do not use cookies

We do not use analytics or tracking technologies

We do not maintain servers that store user data

The Extension operates entirely within the Twitch platform environment and does not transmit user data to external systems.

3. Information from Twitch

The Extension may receive limited contextual information from Twitch necessary for it to function (such as whether it is being viewed on a channel). This information is processed locally within the Twitch Extension environment and is not stored, logged, or retained by us.

All Twitch-related data handling is governed by Twitch’s own policies. Please refer to Twitch’s Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Third-Party Services

If the Extension displays map content or other embedded content from third-party providers, those providers may process data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control or store any such data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by a revised “Effective Date.”
